The name “Khinvasara” is a synonym for trust quality & transparency in Aurangabad. The brand has been involved in modern colonization & real estate development from year 1958. We have long-standing expertise in land development and real Estate Development. We have always believed & adhered to quality, transparency & adding value via our projects. The prime focus of our group is now to diversify into the hospitality & entertainment sectors and take the real estate projects to the next level. Soon we will be launching star-class hotels in & around Aurangabad, amusement parks, multiplexes & luxury resorts.
