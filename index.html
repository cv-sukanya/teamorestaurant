FAQ's

  • What’s the best way to get there by public transport?
    Arriving at Te Amo Veg Restaurant in Aurangabad is a cakewalk with convenient public transport options. Hop on a city bus, catch a cab, or take an auto-rickshaw to reach us hassle-free. With multiple transportation choices, you can focus on savoring our delightful vegetarian cuisine without worrying about the journey. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience at Te Amo Veg Restaurant today!
  • What food does your restaurant specialize in?
    At Te Amo Veg Restaurant, we specialize in crafting a symphony of flavors with our Indian, Chinese, Continental, and Crunchy Comfort Food. Our expert chefs curate each dish with finesse, ensuring a delightful culinary experience for our guests. From traditional Indian delicacies to innovative Chinese creations, we cater to diverse palates while staying true to our commitment to pure vegetarian cuisine. Savor the essence of culinary excellence at Te Amo Veg Restaurant today!
  • Do you use locally sourced ingredients in your recipes?
    Absolutely! At Te Amo Veg Restaurant, we believe in the freshness and quality of our ingredients. While we do source some locally grown produce, we also take pride in importing special ingredients from Mumbai and Pune for our Chinese and Continental dishes. This ensures that every dish we serve is crafted with the finest and freshest ingredients available, promising a delightful dining experience for our guests.
  • Are there any unique or signature dishes I should try?
    At Te Amo Veg Restaurant, we take great pride in offering a unique culinary experience. Indulge in our signature dishes such as the Crunchy Comfort Food, Paneer Makhani, or the irresistible Paneer Tikka Lababdar. For those seeking a taste of the extraordinary, don't miss out on our Smoked Chilli Cottage Cheese or the Classic Thai Veg Green Curry.
